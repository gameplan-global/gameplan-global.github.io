Their privacy policy can be found here . 2.1.2 Using the Service You may store, and we may collect, personal information about your customers as part of your use of the Service. 2.1.3 Contacting Us When you choose to contact GamePlan through the Service, we may collect, store and use the personal information that you provide us, such as your name, email address, phone number and other information you choose to provide in the content of your message, so we can respond to your inquiry. 2.1.4 Device Information We may collect device-specific information (such as your hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers).
