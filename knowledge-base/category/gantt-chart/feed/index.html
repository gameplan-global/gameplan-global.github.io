]]> The Gantt chart shown below the Whiteboard can be exported to a .PDF file for printing and sharing.
Click on the Options menu button at the top of the Gantt chart.

Choose Export to PDF…

A dialog is displayed allowing you to choose the page size, orientation and date range.

Depending on your browser’s settings, you may be asked to open or save the PDF file.

Press OK and GamePlan’s Gantt chart PDF will be displayed.  This is ready to print and to be shared.
