    How is GamePlan different from other project management software?

    You plan your project by drawing your project workflow on the GamePlan Whiteboard. As you plan, the project schedule automatically updates and is displayed in a Gantt chart. Only GamePlan delivers true resource-leveled project scheduling through a visual Whiteboard user experience.

    Beware of task management tools (Asana, Monday, Trello…) that show a Gantt chart. In these tools the start and end dates are entered by the user. This is dangerous because when people see a Gantt chart, they believe it is achievable. When it isnt, due dates are missed and the team is stressed trying to meet unachievable deadlines.

    GamePlans Gantt chart is created by GamePlan’s proprietary algorithms which create a schedule that is optimized and achievable.

    Task Management vs. Project Scheduling

    It’s important to understand the difference between task management tools and project scheduling tools.

    Examples of task management tools are Asana, Monday and Trello. Examples of project management tools are Microsoft Project and GamePlan.

    With task management tools you create tasks and assign task due dates. These tools show when tasks are due, but give no indication if the dates are achievable. When a delay occurs, these tools tell you that you’re late, but they can’t help you further.

    Task management tools work well when a project is NOT time sensitive. However, if time is important, only scheduling tools show you what is achievable and keep you on track to meet the project deliverables.

    Gantt Chart

    A Gantt chart is a bar chart that lets you lay out your project’s schedule. You can do so by outlining your tasks on the vertical axis with respect to time intervals marked on the horizontal axis. A bar is created for every task to represent the start and stop times throughout the duration of the project. This helps you create a horizontal timeline for your projects to determine which projects can be completed simultaneously and ones that must be performed in a particular sequence.

    You can find various free Gantt chart makers online to help you get a comprehensive view of your projects. You can use Gantt charts to understand the duration of multiple tasks, resource usage, and your team’s workload. Gantt chart makers are available online that will automatically project your tasks on the screen as you input the start and stop dates.

    Gantt Scheduling Software

    Gantt charts can add a lot of productivity to your projects. With a powerful Gantt scheduling software, you can furnish your deliverables by knowing exactly when you will deliver tasks, depending on your team size and other variables.

    The schedule is automatically updated and displayed in a Gantt chart as you continue planning. So, if you miss a deadline, you’ll automatically see other things shift on your chart to ensure your team is not trying to meet unachievable deadlines.
